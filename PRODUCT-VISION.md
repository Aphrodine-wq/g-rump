## The Vision

G-Rump isn't just a mascot - **he IS the product**. An animated AI character that creates animations from natural language, with a grumpy but lovable personality that makes the experience memorable and shareable.

## Market Opportunity

- **100M+ content creators** need animated content
- **Existing tools** are complex (After Effects) or limited (Canva)
- **AI chatbots** are text-only and boring
- **No one** has combined AI + Animation + Personality

## The Product

### Core Experience
- Chat with G-Rump (**the same animated character already on your page**)
- Ask for animations in natural language
- G-Rump creates them (with attitude, using his full knowledge base)
- Export to GIF, MP4, Lottie, sprite sheets, code
- Shareable moments (his roasts go viral)

### Important Notes
- **Same Character**: G-Rump AI uses the exact same animated character (15-layer face rig, 16 expressions) that's already on the page
- **Full Knowledge Base**: G-Rump AI has access to the entire knowledge base (animation principles, G-Rump language spec, game dev languages, etc.)
- **Same Personality**: Uses the same G-Rump personality system from `grumpprompt.md`

### G-Rump's Personality States
- **Idle**: "What."
- **Thinking**: Processing your request
- **Annoyed**: "Seriously?"
- **Impressed**: "...okay that's actually not bad"
- **Proud**: "Nailed it. Obviously."
- **Working**: Actually creating your animation

### What He Can Create
- UI/UX animations (loading spinners, button effects)
- Logos & branding (animated logos, intro sequences)
- Social media (animated posts, story animations)
- Game assets (character sprites, particle effects)
- Streaming (alerts, overlays, transitions)
- Presentations (slide transitions, animated charts)
