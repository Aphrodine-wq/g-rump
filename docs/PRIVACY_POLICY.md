# Privacy Policy for Grump

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Welcome to Grump ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Chat Messages**: We collect the messages you send to Grump in order to provide AI-generated responses
- **Conversation History**: We store your conversation history locally on your device using SwiftData (iOS) or similar local storage mechanisms
- **Account Information**: If you subscribe to a paid plan, we collect payment and subscription information through Apple's In-App Purchase system

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Basic device information necessary for app functionality
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the app (locally stored, not transmitted)

### Information from Third-Party Services
- **AI Service Providers**: Your messages are sent to third-party AI providers (Anthropic Claude or Groq) to generate responses. These providers may process your messages according to their own privacy policies.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services
- Generate AI responses to your messages
- Manage your subscription and account
- Monitor usage limits based on your subscription tier
- Improve user experience and app functionality

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Your conversation history is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers
- **Message Processing**: Messages are sent to AI service providers (Anthropic/Groq) for processing but are not permanently stored by these providers beyond the API call
- **Subscription Data**: Subscription information is managed by Apple through their In-App Purchase system

## Third-Party Services

### AI Service Providers
We use the following third-party AI services:
- **Anthropic Claude**: For generating AI responses (when enabled)
- **Groq**: For generating AI responses (when enabled)

These services process your messages to generate responses. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies:
- [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- [Groq Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy)

### Apple Services
- **In-App Purchases**: Subscription management is handled through Apple's In-App Purchase system
- **iCloud** (if enabled): You may optionally sync data across devices using iCloud

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
- **AI Service Providers**: Messages are shared with AI providers to generate responses
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your conversation history stored locally on your device
- **Delete**: Delete your conversation history at any time through the app
- **Export**: Export your conversation data (feature may be added in future updates)
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using the service at any time

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- Local data is stored securely on your device
- We do not store your messages on our servers

## Children's Privacy

Grump is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

## International Users
